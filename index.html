World Happiness Report

The World Happiness Report is a annual publication that assesses the happiness levels of countries worldwide, utilizing data from the Gallup World Poll. The happiness scores are based on respondents' ratings of their current lives on a scale from 0 (worst) to 10 (best).

The data shows a correlation between region's happiness levels and its economy, and economic changes can impact people's perceptions of their happiness.

Each chart displays happiness trend from 2005 to 2022 for a specific region, with annotations at key historical points of potential economic impact on happiness levels in those areas.

Greyout trend lines of other regions in the background provide a comparative context, and hovering highlights the trend line for focus.
